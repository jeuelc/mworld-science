How Did Life Begin?

The origins of life are a fascinating mystery. Most experts believe that 3.5 billion years ago, a 'primordial soup' of molecules was mixing and rearranging itself until POOF, the first cells of life were created! (Primordial means really old from the beginning of time). These single cells are known as 'prokaryotes' and they helped create life as we know it today by creating a new gas in the primitive atmosphere, oxygen.

The story of life's beginning may not be so simple though. We may in fact be... aliens! Yes, that's right, aliens. Another theory, called 'panspermia', suggests that primitive life in the form of bacteria or other kinds of prokaryotes may have hitched a ride to Earth on an asteroid!
