Extinction!

Life hasn't had an easy ride and there have been a few bumps along the way to say the least!

All life on Earth was nearly obliterated five times since the beginning! The most famous and recent of all was the extinction event of the Cretaceous Tertiary extinction event (65 million years ago) which saw the disappearance of the dinosaurs and paved the way for the modern life.

Despite the mass extinction events, there have been some ultimate survivors which still exist today. For those that didn't survive, the gaps, or 'niches' as biologists like to say, that they created allowed new organisms to evolve and create the world anew.
