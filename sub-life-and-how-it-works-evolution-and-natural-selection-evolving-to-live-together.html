Evolution can make some interesting creations! Some evolutionary changes may mean that an organism can live in a new habitat that it wouldn't have survived in previously. Some changes may make animals almost invisible to their predator, and some changes may even cause a new relationship between organisms to form. This could be something like protection from predators in exchange for food or cleaning.

In all the different ways that life has evolved, one thing is for certain: life exists in a fragile balance and every organism in its own unique way has an important part to play. You can see this clearly in food webs. First, microbes break down dead organisms into nutrients for the soil. Nutrients in the soil, combined with the sun, allow plants to grow. Plants support herbivores such as zebras and gazelles. Herbivores in turn provide food for predators such as lions and when ANY organism dies, microbes break them down... and the web continues.
