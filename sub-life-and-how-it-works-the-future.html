The Future

What will life be like in the future? Will people be here? If so, what will we look like in one million years? These questions are fascinating and the answers are limited only by your imagination! We humans have an incredible ability to shape the future of life on Earth. Our intense curiosity to understand the world around us and how it works gives us incredible power to change whole ecosystems. Through the power of conservation, we can help protect and improve all kinds of life like never before.

With the dawn of genetics, our ever-advancing knowledge and technological power has, for the first time in the history of Earth, given humans the ability to directly manipulate life itself. We can potentially create new organisms by directing evolution ourselves and maybe, one day, bring extinct animals back from the dead!
