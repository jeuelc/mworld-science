Are we aliens? Sounds strange and out of this world, but all life on Earth may have originated from a distant planet. This theory is known as panspermia, meaning 'all seeded'.

How? Imagine a planet teeming with simple life like bacteria which has been bit hit by an asteroid. By chance, a piece of that planet carrying bacteria was thrown into space, becoming an interstellar spaceship. But how do bacteria survive in space with no oxygen? And the extreme cold?

Well, believe it or not, on Earth we have organisms that can survive similar conditions. They're called 'extremophiles'; they live and thrive in places that would kill most forms of life. Maybe the extreme aliens hitched a ride until they came crashing into the Earth? A few may have survived, liked it here on Earth and begun to evolve to create life as we know it today.
